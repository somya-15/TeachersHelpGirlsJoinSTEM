About Our Mission

We aim to empower and inspire girls pursuing STEM through AI that provides them a personal mentor, study partner and a motivator.

WHAT'S SPECIAL ABOUT THIS PLATFORM?

This is a website empowered by Computer Vision and Natural Language Based Sentiment Analysis to help assess the emotions and psychology of a child, specially girls when they attempt to solve challenging mathematical problems. Based on the emotions of the child, if she shows signs of under-confidence, irritation or anger, we provide systematic help.

The systematic help is given first through hints to help the child really think deeper. If the emotions don't improve, the system recomments the most helpful and popular solution from the internet so that the child can discover various approaches and methods loved by other learners and doesn't reamin restricted to only one solution!

What We Do?
